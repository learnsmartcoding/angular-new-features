Explanation and Benefits

Reactivity: Using signals ensures that any changes in the cart or the total cost are immediately reflected in the UI without additional manual intervention.

Performance: Signals optimize the reactivity by updating only the parts of the DOM that are affected by changes, leading to more efficient rendering.

Simplicity: Signals provide a straightforward way to manage state and ensure UI consistency, making the code easier to understand and maintain.

Explanation

  • Signal: cart: Tracks the list of items in the shopping cart.
  • Signal: totalCost: Tracks the total cost of items in the cart.
  • Properties:
    • selectedProduct: Holds the currently selected product from the dropdown.
    • quantity: Holds the quantity of the selected product to be added to the cart.
  • Methods:
    • addToCart: Adds the selected product and quantity to the cart or updates the quantity if the product is already in the cart.
    • removeFromCart: Removes a specific item from the cart.
    • updateTotalCost: Calculates and updates the total cost of items in the cart.
    • toggleDetails: Toggles the display of the explanation and benefits section.
  • Template: The template includes a dropdown to select products, an input for quantity, a button to add items to the cart, and a list of cart items with a remove button. It also includes a section to display the total cost and a button to show or hide the explanation and benefits.
